It was of such a kind that the snapping of a pipe-stem too long for him by old Solomon Longways, who was one of those gathered at the shady end of the room, seemed a harsh and irreverent act. Then the ventilator in the window-pane spasmodically started off for a new spin, and the pathos of Donald's song was temporarily effaced.

“'Twas not amiss—not at all amiss!” muttered Christopher Coney, who was also present. And removing his pipe a finger's breadth from his lips, he said aloud, “Draw on with the next verse, young gentleman, please.”
